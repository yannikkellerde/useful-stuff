\section{The better quadratic formula}
Quadratic equation of the form
\begin{equation}
x^2+bx+c = 0
\end{equation}
Get midpoint $m$ between the 2 zero crossings and the distance $d$ between the midpoint and the zero crossings.
\begin{equation}
\begin{aligned}
&m = \frac{b}{2}\\
&d = \sqrt{m^2-c}
\end{aligned}
\end{equation}
Recover solutions:
\begin{equation}
\begin{aligned}
&x^{(1)} = m+d\\
&x^{(2)} = m-d
\end{aligned}
\end{equation}
